The Agent's Architecture

The "Plan & Reflect" Loop

At the heart of the agent is an intelligent loop that mimics how a human analyst thinks. It's not just a simple question-to-SQL converter; it's a genuine research partner.

  • Plan: Based on your goal and the data it has, the agent plans the next logical analytical step.
  • Act: It writes and executes a targeted SQL query against your database to gather new information.
  • Reflect: It analyzes the results of its query and decides if it has enough information to answer your overall question, or if it needs to plan another step.
